Services We Offer

Crime Scene Cleanup

Safe and thorough removal of biohazards, blood, and forensic materials left behind at crime scenes. We follow law enforcement release protocols and use professional-grade disinfectants to return the property to a safe condition.

Trauma & Accident Cleanup

Comprehensive cleanup after traumatic incidents such as falls, workplace injuries, or assaults. We disinfect all affected areas and dispose of contaminated materials in compliance with California regulations.

Unattended Death & Decomposition Cleanup

Professional decontamination and odor removal after a person passes away and is not discovered for some time. This includes bodily fluid cleanup, structural assessment, and use of specialized disinfectants to address bacteria and odor.

Suicide & Homicide Cleanup

Respectful and discreet biohazard cleaning services following suicides or violent deaths. We ensure the complete removal of bloodborne pathogens and restore the space with dignity and care.

Blood & Bodily Fluid Cleanup

Thorough disinfection of any environment exposed to blood, vomit, urine, or other bodily fluids. We eliminate pathogens, treat stains, and return the area to a safe, habitable condition.

Biohazard Cleanup & Disposal

Removal, containment, and proper disposal of any biohazardous materials. We comply with OSHA and state standards to ensure safe handling and documentation of all waste.

Infectious Disease Cleaning

Disinfection of areas exposed to infectious diseases such as COVID-19, Hepatitis, or MRSA. We use EPA-registered disinfectants to reduce contamination and help prevent the spread of illness.

Tear Gas & Pepper Spray Cleanup

Neutralization of chemical agents following law enforcement deployment. We clean walls, air systems, and surfaces to remove irritants and restore breathable air quality.

Hoarding & Clutter Cleanup

Cleaning and restoring homes affected by hoarding, which may include hidden biohazards such as mold, waste, or rodent droppings. We handle each job with discretion and respect for the occupant's dignity.

Urine & Feces Cleanup

Disinfection of areas contaminated by human or animal waste. We use strong disinfectants to eliminate bacteria and lingering odors, making the space safe again.

Homeless Encampment Cleanup

Removal of biohazards, waste, and debris from encampment sites. Our team safely clears sharp objects, soiled bedding, and contaminated items while disinfecting the area.

Animal Waste & Dead Animal Cleanup

Cleanup of feces, urine, and carcasses from animals in residential, commercial, or public areas. We eliminate contaminants, address odor issues, and remove any health hazards left behind.

Odor Removal & Deodorization

Permanent removal of odors caused by decomposition, waste, or other biohazards. We use commercial-grade deodorizers, foggers, and ozone treatments to neutralize air and surfaces.

Industrial Accident Cleanup

Biohazard cleanup after workplace accidents involving blood or bodily fluids. We ensure complete disinfection and safe disposal while minimizing disruption to operations.

Vehicle Biohazard Cleaning

Full disinfection and decontamination of vehicles affected by biohazards like blood, vomit, or waste. We restore the interior safely and discreetly using industry-approved procedures.

Service Locations

BioSecure Solutions is proud to serve communities across Southern California with trusted biohazard cleanup and remediation services. Our licensed team is strategically located to provide fast, discreet response wherever you need us.
