About Us

BioSecure Solutions provides professional crime scene and biohazard cleanup across Southern California. Fully OSHA-certified and licensed (TSW #863), we ensure every site is remediated safely, discreetly, and in full compliance with regulations. With unmarked vehicles, rapid response, and a respectful approach, we're here when you need us most.

Infectious Disease Cleaning

Disinfection of areas exposed to infectious diseases such as COVID-19, Hepatitis, or MRSA. We use EPA-registered disinfectants to reduce contamination and help prevent the spread of illness.
